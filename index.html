Frequently Asked Questions

For more specific questions, please contact Mr. Swope at swopej@leonschools.net or email your questions to chilesicc@gmail.com

  • How do I start a club?

    Please follow the guidelines from above.

  • Where will ICC meetings be held?

    In normal circumstances, ICC meetings will be held in the conference on the second floor of the office. In abnormal circumstances, ICC meetings will be held virtually via Zoom. Join the ICC remind to get the meeting link then.

  • How often will meetings be held?

    ICC meetings will usually be held once a month, usually the first Tuesday of every month.

  • Are the meetings mandatory?

    Please try to attend all meetings! If there is no representative from a club for more than two meetings in a semester, that club will be removed from ICC.

Chiles High School

Lawton Chiles High School opened its doors in August 1999, welcoming 767 freshman and sophomore students from Leon County's northeast region. By the time the first senior class graduated in May of 2002, the school's population was just over 1,600 students. Our current enrollment is just over 2,000 students.
